Welcome to the **Introduction to Enterprise Computing** course, brought to you by **IBM**! This foundational course will introduce you to the core concepts, systems, and technologies that power enterprise-scale computing, particularly on IBM Z mainframes. With mainframes responsible for the majority of global business transactions, understanding their architecture and operation is critical for anyone aspiring to work in IT infrastructure, systems engineering, or enterprise-level security.

---
## 🎯 What You’ll Learn

By the end of this course, you will be able to:
- **Identify Architectural Families**: Understand the evolution of mainframe architectures and the key enhancements that differentiate each generation.    
- **Understand Operating System Functions**: Learn the role of operating systems in enterprise environments, with a focus on **z/OS**.    
- **Explore Sysplex Concepts**: Explain the Sysplex architecture and how it supports scalability, availability, and reliability.    
- **Implement z/OS Security Methods**: Gain insights into security protocols and features available for enterprise systems.    
- **Define IOCP Statements & Activation Profiles**: Learn how to describe and configure IOCP (Input/Output Configuration Program) statements, and understand activation profiles for system startup.
